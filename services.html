My services

I work as a physiotherapist and coach at Hospital Ribera IMSKE, Valencia. My specialties in physiotherapy are rehabilitation for the knee, for all kinds of injuries or pain. I work both with conservative treatment as well as rehab after a surgery for ACL-injuries and meniscal injuries. I also have a lot of experience in treating tendinopathies and other knee pain. My other specialty is CrossFit and weightlifting. With the help of a throughout interview and functional exploration, I make a personalized rehabilitation plan with the patient for any kinds of injuries or pain associated with CrossFit or weightlifting. My focus is in active physiotherapy and exercise. I also work with anyone who wishes to train to improve their health, or improve their performance in sports. I work in MyPowerFix as a online coach. I currently offer 5 different programs for reducing the risk of injury and improving performance and gymnastic skills in CrossTraining.

MPF MOBILITY is a program designed to improve your mobility for crosstraining with active movements. Daily training time is 10-15min, and most of the workouts can easily be performed at home. MPF ALL-IN-ONE-ACCESSORIES is a program designed to improve your control, stability and mobility for crosstraining, as well as help prevent injuries. Daily training time is 10-15min, and most of the workouts can easily be performed at home. You get access to the FITR APP, where you can find the instructions and videos, as well as help from the coach if needed. I also offer 3 different gymnastics programs:

MPF FIRST HSPU is an 8-week long course during which we will improve the strength, mobility and control needed for handstand push ups. The goal is also to get a strict HSPU, either all the way to the floor or to an abmat. The course includes 2x30min workouts per week and an extra optional workout for mobility + CORE. The workouts include a warm up (mobility and control), a strength-part and an accessory workout in the end (strength + control). The workouts are progressive and varied. To make sure you get everything out of the course, we recommend you to be able to get up to a handstand hold by the wall and to be able to perform 5-10 strict normal push ups. This course can easily be combined with other training, but we recommend you to start your training day with the program of the course to assure good quality.

In the MPF KIPPING GYMNASTICS LEVEL 1-program we practice the strength, skill and control needed for all hanging kipping gymnastics movements. We will practice an efficient kip swing overall, and skill and later on capacity of the kipping pull up and kipping toes to bar. The program includes 2x 15-20min workouts each week. The workouts can easily be combined with another training program, or be done before or after a group class. The goal is to be able to do several repetitions of the kipping pull up and kipping toes to bar with a good technique at the end of the 12-week program. All workouts include a warm up, a skill/capacity-workout and an accessory/strength-workout. This course is a good base for all kipping movements, and after completing it, even the more advanced kipping movements can be practiced more easily and safely.

This is a 12-week program that includes 3 four-week training cycles:

  • CYCLE 1: the skill and strength of the kip swing, core control and strength, strict pulling strength
  • CYCLE 2: kipping pull up skill practice, kipping t2b skill practice, core control and strength, strict pulling strength progressing from cycle 1
  • CYCLE 3: kipping pull up-capacity, kipping t2b-capacity, core control and strength, strict pulling strength progressing from cycle 1 and 2
FOR WHO? this course is suitable for you if you can hang in the bar, can do at least 3 strict pull ups and are able to lift your legs straight up to 90 degress at least 5 times while hanging from the bar. It's for anyone that wants to improve the efficiency and technique of all hanging kipping movements, and build a strong base for the more advanced kipping movements.

MPF FIRST RMU

Do you want to learn how to do yout first RMU? Or can you already get a few reps, but you want to improve your technique? This program is a perfect choice!

It's a 12-week program, that includes 2 workouts a week / 30-45min each workout. All workouts include a warm up, a skill-piece and a strength-/accessory-piece. We recommend to do this workout before the main workout, so that you still have a fresh body and can focus fully.

NOTE! The requirement for participating is to get 3-5 strict pull ups and 3-5 strict dips, so that we can guarantee the strength levels and prevent injuries.
