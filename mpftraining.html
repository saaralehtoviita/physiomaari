MPF-training

MPF COMPETE is a training program for functional fitness. We offer 3 different programs:

  • MPF COMPETE (includes access to MPF COMPETE & MPF COMPETE 90-track)
  • MPF COMPETE 3+1
  • MPF COMPETE PRO
MPF COMPETE includes 5 main tranining sessions a week + an optional mobility or zone 2+mobility workout on Thursdays. The daily training time is 90-120min with some extra optional workouts each week. MPF COMPETE 90 is a reduced version with max 90min daily training time. MPF COMPETE 3+1 includes 3 main training sessions a week + an optional session. Daily training time is 90-120min. MPF COMPETE PRO includes 10 main training sessions per week, divided into 5 training days, + an optional mobility workout on Thursdays. Each session lasts 60-120min, and the daily training time is between 2.5-3 hours. This program option is designes for those who have more time and resources for training and want to dedicate more time to improving their fitness.

The goal of MPF COMPETE-programs is to improve your strength, endurance, weightlifting, gymnastics, mobility and skills, no matter your fitness level. Each training day includes a specific warm up, the main workout, accessories and possibly an optional piece. The main workout includes 3-4 pieces, that are either weightlifting, strength, skill, gymnastics, movement quality or endurance. The optional piece can be any one of these as well, or an accessory-piece.

MYPOWERFIX Longevity is one of our main focuses in the program. This is taken into account in the programming when planning the specific loading for each joint throught the week, but also with our warm ups and accessories. The warm ups and accessories are planned by a MyPowerFix physiotherapist. The warm ups include specific mobility and activation work for the upcoming session, to prepare your body as well as possible. We also work on the typical weaker links we find in athletes, as well as skill work. The accessories include sport specific movements, but we also work on the things that are left out in the sports. A healthy joint needs strength, stability and mobility in all movement directions and in all ranges of motion. We make sure to provide a varied accessory program to complement the typical functional fitness-movements, to make stronger and more stable joints, and to reduce the risk of injury

PERIODIZATION

To be able to maximize performance and progress, we work in 4 main training cycles throughout the year. Currently the CrossFit Open & Quarterfinals are the main goals during the season, and we build towards them throughout the year. Even though every training cycle has its own focus points, we do train everything all year around, and always aim to stay in good shape!

INDIVIDUALIZATION

We have wanted to create a program that works for all different levels of athletes. Always when needed, the workouts and gymnastics progressions have 3 different levels (LEVEL 1: Elite / international level athlete, LEVEL 2: RX / national level athlete, LEVEL 3: Intermediate/scaled level athlete). This allows you to always get the correct stimulus of each workout, and you don't have to think yourself about how to scale the workout. Each day also includes an optional piece for those who have more time and resources for training. Some days we also offer different options for the workouts, depending on what you want to work on specifically. Feel free to ask us any questions if you want more information about which level to choose, and remember we also offer the semipersonalized option for those who want to work on some specific parts of the training with their own coach, while still following our main track.

The semipersonalized training programs includes access to one of the COMPETE-programs, with me as a personal coach. Together with me, we analyze your current situation and make goals for you. You also pick 2-3 things you want to work on, and the normal training program is adjusted to you, based on your goals and current training situation. You also receive video feedback weekly from me, as well as help for qualifiers and competitions.

The personalized training program is a 100% personalized training program based on your goals and current situation. You also receive video feedback weekly from me, as well as help for qualifiers and competitions.
